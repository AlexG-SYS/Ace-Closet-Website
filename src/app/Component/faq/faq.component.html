FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Please reach us at info@AceCloset.bz if you cannot find an answer to your question.

1) How do I create an account?
Please click the Register button located at the top-right corner of the page. Follow the instructions and fill in all the required fields. Once your account is created, you will receive an automated message on WhatsApp.
2) What payment methods do you accept?
We accept eKyash, Digi Wallet, Visa/Mastercard Debit and Credit Cards. As well as Direct Bank Deposit.
3) How can I track my order?
All orders are shipped using the courier service selected at checkout. Once your order has been shipped, the courier will provide tracking updates.

6) Can I make changes to my order after placing it?
Yes, you can make changes to your order within 30 minutes of placing it. After that, the order will be processed and no further changes can be made.
7) What are your shipping options?
We provide country-wide shipping with BPMS, IDS, Ezy and the Post Office.
8) How long will my order take to arrive?
The delivery time depends on the courier service you selected at checkout. You will receive an estimated delivery date at checkout.
9) How can I change my shipping address?
You can change your shipping address under the Settings section of your user profile. However, if an order has already passed the 30-minute modification window, the shipping address cannot be changed for that order.
10) What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us via WhatsApp at 628-9704, and we will resolve the issue promptly.
